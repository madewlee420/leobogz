Leobog Hi75

The LEOBOG A75 features Alice layout with function row, designed to provide a natural and relaxed hand and finger position for a more comfortable typing experience. This layout is favored by both everyday users and keyboard enthusiasts for its ergonomic benefits, particularly during long typing sessions.

The top right corner houses a multifunctional knob for volume and RGB brightness adjustment (Long press to switch between 2 functions), which can be customized to your preference using the Leobog driver. It provides a satisfying clicky sound and tactile feedback when turned.
